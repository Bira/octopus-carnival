The Grineer are one of the main enemy factions in _Warframe_. They're what GURPS
calls bioroids: mass-produced people built entirely through bioengineering
supplemented by cybernetics.

They were originally built by a civilization of decadent transhumans who called
themselves the Orokin, for use as manual laborers working in mines and
factories. When some of the Orokin's other creations rebelled against them, the
Grineer were turned into soldiers.

Those rebels could quickly take over any of the smart sci-fi weapons the Orokin
had available, so the Grineer were given "dumb" weapons operating on archaic
principles but built with modern engineering. They stuck with these after the
Orokin fell, which is a story for another time.

Grineer equipment, just like the Grineer themselves, is designed with rapid
mass-production in mind and tends to have a rounded, bulbous look. It comes out
in large numbers from automated factories that use the simplest processes and
the loosest tolerances they can get away with. That said, it incorporates some
impressive materials engineering, and they can also make more advanced weapons
for use by elite units.

Ranged weapons are chemical slugthrowers and tend to lack stocks, relying on
their users' great strength and heavy armor to absorb the recoil. Melee weapons
use physical blades and striking surfaces, usually enhanced by some
mechanism. Armor tends to be quite heavy and strong, but rarely powered.
