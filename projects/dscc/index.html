Let's Read the Dark Sun Creature Catalog

RPG.net forum user zedturtle decided to do a reading of the Dark Sun Campaign Setting book, and I decided to do a parallel one of the Dark Sun Creature Catalog. There were the only two books published for Dark Sun during D&D Fourth Edition: the Campaign Setting book also included player-facing material.

This page collects all posts related to that project.
